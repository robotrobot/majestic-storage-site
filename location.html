Our Location

Secure, discrete, convenient

The map below shows the location of our sister company's workshop, Majestic Motors.

We are conveniently located between Birmingham, Coventry and Leamington Spa, Birmingham Airport, the M42 & M40 motorways, Birmingham International & Coventry train station are all just a few minutes away.

Here, we meet and greet new and existing customers and prepare cars for storage before we carefully transport them to our nearby facility, 3 miles away. This keeps the location of your vehicle whilst in our care discrete.
